Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients
500g spaghetti
400g minced beef
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
400g canned tomatoes
2 tbsp tomato paste
1 tsp dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste

Details

  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Servings: 4
  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 30 minutes

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  2. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  6. Serve the spaghetti with the Bolognese sauce on top. Enjoy!
